GAME SUMMARY

You wake up inside a abandoned building. You don’t recall how you got there. You have a notebook in your hand, with a note on the cover. The note reads: “OLA, let’s play a game. Try to find me on the roof, if you can.....” Each door leading to the top floor is locked. To open the door you must answer questions on various devices, such as phones and tablets hidden on the room. Answer correctly to open the door. Along the way find clues to what is going on. But be careful, it appears you are being watched.
